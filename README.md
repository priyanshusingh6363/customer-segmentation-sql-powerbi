# 🛍 Customer Segmentation Analysis using SQL + Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior and segments customers based on **Annual Income** and **Spending Score**.

The goal is to help businesses understand different customer groups and improve marketing strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Identify high-value customers
- Understand spending patterns
- Create customer personas
- Improve targeted marketing campaigns
- Increase customer retention and revenue

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- SQL
- Power BI
- Excel / CSV
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics

---

## 📂 Dataset Features
- CustomerID
- Gender
- Age
- Annual Income
- Spending Score

---

## 📊 SQL Analysis Performed
### 1. Customer KPIs
- Total Customers
- Gender Distribution
- Average Age
- Average Income
- Average Spending Score

### 2. Customer Segmentation Logic
- VIP → High income + high spending
- Cautious → High income + low spending
- Bargain Hunter → Low income + high spending
- Occasional Buyer → Low income + low spending

### 3. Age Groups
- Young
- Adult
- Senior

### 4. Income Tiers
- Low
- Medium
- High

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights
- Total Customers KPI

- Income Tier Analysis
- Age Group Distribution
- Segment Breakdown
- Top 5 Customers by Spending Score
- Spending by Gender

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- VIP customers are the most profitable segment
- Bargain Hunters spend highly despite lower income
- Cautious customers need premium conversion strategies
- Adult age group contributes the highest customer count

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Launch loyalty campaigns for VIP customers
- Personalized offers for Bargain Hunters
- Upsell premium products to Cautious customers
- Retention campaigns for Occasional Buyers
